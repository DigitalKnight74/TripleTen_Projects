## 📌 Project Overview

Developed a prototype ML model for **Zyfra**, a company specializing in industrial efficiency solutions for heavy industry. The model predicts the amount of gold recovered from gold ore using process data from the extraction and purification stages.

Accurate prediction of gold recovery helps eliminate unprofitable processing configurations, improve operational decision-making, and support optimization of the production pipeline.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze and preprocess multi-stage industrial process data
- Build regression models to predict gold recovery at rougher and final stages
- Evaluate models using a custom symmetric Mean Absolute Percentage Error (sMAPE) metric
- Identify the best-performing model for production use

- Cleaned and merged multi-stage process data across rougher and final stages
- Random Forest and XGBoost models evaluated and compared
- Final model selected based on lowest sMAPE score across both prediction stages
