## 📌 Project Overview

Worked for the OilyGiant mining company to find the best location for a new oil well. Using oil sample data from three regions, built a regression model to predict reserve volumes and applied Bootstrapping to analyze potential profit and investment risk.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Collect and analyze oil well parameters across three regions
- Build a regression model to predict volume of reserves in new wells
- Select the top oil wells with the highest estimated values per region
- Identify the region with the highest total profit margin
- Use Bootstrapping to quantify risk and confidence intervals

## 📊 Key Results

- Linear Regression models trained and evaluated for all three regions
- Bootstrapping with 1,000 samples used to estimate profit distributions and risk
- Identified the optimal region balancing expected profit and acceptable risk of loss
